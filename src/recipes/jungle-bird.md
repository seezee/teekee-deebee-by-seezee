## Ingredients

- 1 ounce pineapple juice
- 1/2 ounce fresh lime juice
- 1/2 ounce Demerara syrup
- 3/4 ounces Campari
- 1&frac12; ounces black blended rum<icon-l space="1em"><span class="with-icon"><svg class="icon"><use href="/assets/images/icons/circle-5.svg#circle-5"></use></svg><span class="sr-only">(5)</span></span></icon-l>

Garnish
  : Pineapple fronds

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients + 12 ounces crushed ice + 4 to 6 small cubes to a drink mixer tin
2. Flash blend 10 seconds
3. Open pour with a gated finish into a Collins or highball glass
4. Insert 3 or more pineapple fronds in the glass
