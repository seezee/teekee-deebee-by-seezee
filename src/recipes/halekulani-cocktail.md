## Ingredients

* &frac12; oz. fresh lemon juice
* &frac12; oz. fresh orange juice
* &frac12; oz. fresh pineapple juice
* &frac14; oz. rich demerara syrup
* &frac12; oz. grenadine
* 1&frac12; oz. bourbon
* 1 dash Angostura bitters

Garnish
  : Edible orchid

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with cracked or cubed ice
2. Shake for 10 to 15 seconds
3. Double-strain into a chilled coupé
4. Garnish with an edible orchid on the side of the glass
