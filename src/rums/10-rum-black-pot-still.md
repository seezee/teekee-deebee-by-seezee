* Hamilton Jamaica Black (Jamaica)

</div>

<tiki-callout type="note">

Hamilton Jamaica Gold and Hamilton Jamaica Black are identical except for the type of caramel coloring used. Gold uses gold tint caramel from [Sethness Roquette](https://www.sethness-roquette.com/){target=_blank rel="external noopener"} while Black uses double strength black caramel made from American sugar, also by Sethness.[^1]

Therefore, Hamilton Jamaica Black is technically an unaged pot still rum.

Regarding taste and aroma, importer Edward Hamilton says there is no significant difference between the two rums.[^2]
